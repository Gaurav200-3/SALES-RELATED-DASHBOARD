# This Sales Dashboard – Monthly is designed to provide a time-based (monthly & daily) analysis of sales performance, along with insights into categories and customers. It’s ideal for tracking short-term trends and operational performance.


# 1. Left Panel – Navigation
Dashboard / Details buttons
Used to switch between summary view and detailed analysis page

👉 Keeps reporting structured and user-friendly

# 2. Top Right – Date Filter (Year, Month)
Allows selection of:
Specific year
Specific month

👉 All visuals update dynamically → enables time-based analysis

# 3. KPI Cards (Top Summary Metrics)
Sum of Sales → 20K
Sum of Price → 12K
Sum of Discount → 324%
Sum of Quantity → 196
🔍 Insights:
Moderate sales volume (20K)
Discounts are relatively high → could impact profit
Quantity sold (196 units) indicates transaction volume

# 4. Sales & Sub-Category Count by Day (Bar Chart)
What it shows:
Daily sales performance
Alongside count of sub-categories sold
Insights:
Sales fluctuate daily
Some clear spikes (~2K) → high-demand days
Many low days → inconsistent demand

👉 Useful for:

Identifying peak sales days
Planning promotions or inventory

# 5. Sales Trend by Day (Line Chart)
Shows daily sales trend across months
Observations:
Peaks around:
Mid-January
End of January
Early February
Several dips close to zero

👉 Insight:

Sales are highly volatile
Not a smooth growth pattern → needs stabilization

# 6. Sales by Category (Donut Chart)

Categories:

Technology (~54%) → highest
Furniture (~40%)
Office Supplies (~5%) → lowest
Insights:
Technology dominates revenue
Office supplies contribute very little

👉 Business action:

Focus marketing on high-performing categories
Improve low-performing category strategy

# 7. Customer Sales Table (Right Bottom)
Lists Customer ID vs Sales
Example:
C2005 → highest (~1800)
C2001 → ~1600
Others → lower contributions
Insights:
Few customers generate most revenue
Indicates customer concentration

👉 Action:

Retain top customers (loyalty programs)
Increase engagement for low-value customers

# Key Insights Summary

Sales are volatile day-to-day
Technology category dominates
Discounts are relatively high
Few customers contribute most revenue
Some days generate significant spikes

# Final Summary

This dashboard is a monthly performance tracker that helps:

Monitor daily sales fluctuations

Understand category contribution

Identify top customers

Evaluate discount impact

It’s especially useful for sales managers to make short-term decisions like promotions, inventory planning, and customer targeting.
